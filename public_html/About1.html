About Us

The Arrighetto family traveled to America in 1846. Through tough trials and strong backs they established themselves on the East Coast in the wonderful state of New Jersey. Their initial berth into the West was full of ups and downs. Finally in 1955 Alfonzo Arrighetto grew tired of the family tradition of hard labor and decided to venture into the wonderful world of fine dining. He traveled all around New Jersey in search of other Italian families, to find the greatest recipes straight from his home country! In 1956 Alfonzo and his three sons Mario, Vinny, and Frankie broke ground on the construction of Arrighetto's Pizzeria. Less than a year later the ovens were hot, and the pizzas flew from the doors faster than the family could make them. Business has, since that day, been astoundingly successful and will continue to for some time. Today we are still providing some of the greatest Italian food this side of the Delaware River! All of our ingredients come fresh from local farmers. Please stop and visit us today!
